Scrambled eggs

Easy, Quick scrambled eggs recipe

Quick, easy source of quality protein meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbs of butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • cream or milk
  • cheese

Recipe Instruction

  1. Pre-heat your pan and 1 tbs of butter and wait until it melts and heats up
  2. Crack the 3 eggs and drop it in the pan
  3. Pour in a spash of milk
  4. Add salt, black pepper, any spices and herbs and stir it all together.
  5. Add Cheese and mix it in the cooked egg
  6. Once finished, pour it all in a plate and enjoy
