Iced Coffee

Easy, Quick cold coffee recipe

This drink may be drink black or with milk and/or creamer.

It can be sweetened with honey, syrup, sugar, stevia etc

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffe Powder or Coffe of your choice
  • Coconut milk or any milk or cream of your choice
  • Stevia, honey, or sweetner of your choice
  • Optional - Protein Powder

Recipe Instruction

  1. Pour milk first then coffee, sweetner and protein powder in a blender
  2. Blend it all for 30 seconds
  3. Pour in a cup or glass and enjoy
