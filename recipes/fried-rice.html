Fried Rice

Easy, Quick fried rice recipe

Recipe when you feel lazy and you have leftover cold rice.

Ingredients

  • Cold White Rice
  • 1 tbs of butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 Eggs
  • Mixed Veggies

Recipe Instruction

  1. Pre-heat your pan and 1 tbs of butter and wait until it melts and heats up
  2. Throw your mixed veggies and let it sit there until it's soft and juicy
  3. Pour in eggs and let it cook
  4. Pour in your cold white rice and salt, black pepper, any spices and herbs and stir it all together.
  5. Once finished, pour it all in a plate and enjoy
